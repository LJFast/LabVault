# Reference Ranges (Population)

These are typical adult population reference ranges, useful as a sanity check or fallback when a result entry doesn't carry its own range. **Always prefer the range printed on the actual lab report**, which the `range:` field on each result entry captures.

Ranges below are commonly cited values for adults; they may not match every lab's specific assay. For age-, sex-, or assay-dependent ranges, consult the source.

## CKD staging quick reference (KDIGO 2024)

GFR categories:

- G1: ≥90 (normal or high)
- G2: 60–89 (mildly decreased)
- G3a: 45–59 (mildly to moderately decreased)
- G3b: 30–44 (moderately to severely decreased)
- G4: 15–29 (severely decreased)
- G5: <15 (kidney failure)

Albuminuria categories (UACR mg/mmol):

- A1: <3 (normal to mildly increased)
- A2: 3–30 (moderately increased)
- A3: >30 (severely increased)
